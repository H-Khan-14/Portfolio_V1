BIO

My Story

From a very young age I have had a fascination with technology, how with a few lines of code it enables you to create something entirely beautiful and unique. How onecan create applications that have unique characters, images, landscapes and effects with endless possibilities. The ability to essentially bring your imagination, your creativity to life is nothing less than magic. I never would have thought to make a career out of it but my brother showed me coding firsthand, and I was just as captivated now as I was before. The ability to type a few lines of code and create something is an amazing experience, to look at words, symbols and numbers and see the kinds of changes they can make. To see the direct cause and effect of each action. It’s an experience that I love and hope to make a lifetime career of.
